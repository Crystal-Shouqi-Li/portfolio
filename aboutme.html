Macbeth: 1, Reckless Youngsters: 0 MACBETH: My name's Macbeth. YOUNG SIWARD: The devil himself could not pronounce a title More hateful to mine ear. MACBETH: No, nor more fearful. YOUNG SIWARD: Thou liest, abhorred tyrant; with my sword I'll prove the lie thou speak'st. [They fight, and young Seward is slain.] MACBETH: Thou wast born of woman.-- But swords I smile at, weapons laugh to scorn, Brandish'd by man that's of a woman born.
